Linear Regression

Model (line):

\( \hat{y} = m \cdot x + b \)

Loss (Mean Squared Error):

\( \text{MSE} = \frac{1}{n} \sum_{i=1}^{n} (y_i - \hat{y}_i)^2 \)

Gradient Descent Update:

\( m \leftarrow m - \alpha \cdot \frac{\partial \text{MSE}}{\partial m}, \quad b \leftarrow b - \alpha \cdot \frac{\partial \text{MSE}}{\partial b} \)
